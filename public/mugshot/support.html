Getting Help

If Mugshot is not behaving as expected, please include:

  • Your iPhone or iPad model.
  • Your iOS version.
  • What you were trying to do.
  • What happened instead.

Common Questions

Does Mugshot require an account?

No. Mugshot stores your coffee log locally on your device.

Does Mugshot sync with Apple Health?

Apple Health caffeine sync is optional. Turn it on from Settings if you want Mugshot to write caffeine amount and time to Apple Health.

Can I use photos instead of the camera?

Yes. You can import a coffee photo from your Photo Library.

How do I back up my Mugshot data?

Open Settings, then use the data export option to create a JSON backup that includes your entries, originals, and stickers.
